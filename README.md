## 🧩 What is KREASYS?

KREASYS is a browser-based AI ecosystem and development environment. It lets you run AI tools directly on your Windows computer without sending data to the internet. This approach keeps your information private and safe.

You can interact using text, images, audio, and video. It supports local AI models that run with your computer’s graphics hardware. KREASYS also stores your files inside the app, so your work stays with you.

The app works fully in your browser, no installation of complex software is needed.

---

## 💻 System Requirements

Before you start, check that your Windows PC meets these needs:

- Windows 10 or later.
- A browser that supports WebGPU. The latest version of Microsoft Edge or Chrome is recommended.
- A computer with a graphics card that supports WebGPU (roughly any GPU from the last 3 years).
- At least 8 GB of RAM.
- Stable internet connection for the initial download.

## 📂 Using the Virtual File System (VFS)

KREASYS uses a built-in file storage system. It works inside the app and does not save files to your regular folders unless you export them.

- To create a new file, click "New File" inside the app.
- Save your progress by clicking the save icon. Your work stays within the app.
- To export files to your PC, use the "Export" option.
- Import files using the "Import" button to bring your work into KREASYS.

All files are kept private and do not leave your computer without your permission.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

- **App won’t start or crashes**: Ensure your browser supports WebGPU and your GPU drivers are up to date.
- **AI models load slowly**: Local hardware resources affect speed. Closing other programs may help.
- **Files missing or lost**: Make sure to regularly export needed files outside the app.
- **Network errors on first launch**: The app may need internet access to download AI models or updates.
